Mommy & Me Yoga

Tuesdays on February 3, 17, March 3, 17
$60 for the four week session, or $20 drop in

Are you a new mom who wants to get back on the mat but can’t find just the right place for you to practice? Well look no more... join us on the 1st and 3rd Tuesday's of the month A Touch of Health starting in February, with your delicious bundle in tow. There will be other mommies there who want to practice too and don’t mind if you have to stop to check in on your baby or if your baby needs a cuddle or some attention. It is a great way to get out of the house and meet some new moms and babes for you to connect with, laugh with, and share your experiences.
