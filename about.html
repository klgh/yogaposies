About Natalie Leach

Meet Natalie

Natalie Leach E-RYT200, RCYT began her yoga practice in June 2000 and has been hooked ever since.

In February 2005, she earned her teacher training through Peachtree Yoga Center; and has completed both the Anusara Immersion and Teacher training’s with Atlanta’s own Gina Minyard.

Natalie considers herself both a teacher and a student, and she continues to deepen her practice through classes, workshops, and meditation.

Natalie has studied with nationally recognized teachers such as Ross Rayburn, Martin Kirk, Christina Sell, Desiree Rumbaugh Doug Keller, Tias Little, Amy Weintraub, and YogaKids founder Marsha Wenig. In addition to her 200 hour Yoga Alliance certification, Natalie has completed the YogaKids 310 hour certification, and is Grounded Kids Certified. Her instruction is rooted in her many years of Anusara study and trainings. Her adult classes have been described as challenging, and fun.

Mother of three, Natalie has a special interest in teaching kids yoga. Her playful approach inspires a love for the physical practice of yoga while making it fun. Her goal is to help kids see with their hearts, both on and off the mat, and to realize their full potential.

Natalie is also a Power Pilates Beginner/Intermediate Mat Teacher.

Check out Natalie's book, 'What We Know' available on Amazon Kindle!
